Child Safety Standards and Protection Policy

Zero-Tolerance Policy

We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy regarding child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). This policy outlines our commitment to preventing, detecting, and reporting any form of child exploitation or abuse on our platform.

Preventive Measures

Age Verification

  • Mandatory age verification during registration
  • Photo ID verification for all users
  • Artificial intelligence-powered age estimation technology
  • Regular account audits and verification checks

Content Monitoring

  • Automated content scanning using AI and machine learning
  • Real-time image and video screening
  • Text analysis for suspicious patterns and conversations
  • Proactive monitoring of user interactions

Detection and Enforcement

Automated Detection Systems

  • Advanced AI algorithms to detect suspicious behavior patterns
  • Image recognition technology to screen for inappropriate content
  • Behavioral analysis to identify potential predatory actions
  • Network pattern analysis to detect organized abuse

User Reporting System

  • Easily accessible reporting buttons on all content
  • Emergency reporting option for immediate threats
  • In-app safety center with reporting guidelines
  • Anonymous reporting options

Response Protocol

Immediate Actions

  1. Instant account suspension upon credible report
  2. Evidence preservation for law enforcement
  3. Removal of suspicious content
  4. Block of associated devices and IP addresses

Partnerships and Cooperation

Law Enforcement Collaboration

  • Dedicated law enforcement liaison team
  • Streamlined process for data requests
  • Regular coordination meetings
  • Rapid response protocols
